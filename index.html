Things We’ve Learnt from You

  • Discipline: You always keep your habits — taking a morning shower, praying, or doing Suryanamaskar before eating. The time may change, but you never miss it.

  • Contentment: You’ve taught us that it’s good to dream and improve, but never be jealous of others.
  • Forgiveness: You have a big heart and always choose to forgive and move on, even when something hurtful is said. You stay the same kind and calm person — that’s special.
  • Kindness: It’s amazing how you remember and pray for so many people. It shows your kind and good heart.

And of course, we can’t forget your love for food! You’ve always shown how to enjoy a meal like a king and serve others with care. And yes — your little dessert tricks are the best: “No no, I’ve only had one... okay maybe one last time!” 😄

Dad, you’ve taught us how to live happily, stay grounded, and keep a big heart. Thank you for always being strong, kind, and wise.

Here’s to You!
