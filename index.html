🎉 Happy 76th Birthday, Dad! 🎉

Wishing you a very happy birthday filled with good health and happiness. You have truly lived life in your own way — with confidence and peace — and that is something I really admire. You have taught me many valuable lessons — how to stay humble (something I am still learning from you), how to be happy with what we have, and how to only compete with ourselves — to become better, not to envy others.

Things We’ve Learnt from You

  • Confidence: You always believe in yourself. Even now, your famous line makes us smile — “Don’t worry, I will manage.”

  • Contentment: You’ve taught us that it’s good to dream and improve, but never be jealous of others.
  • Forgiveness: You have a big heart and always choose to forgive and move on, even when something hurtful is said. You stay the same kind and calm person — that’s special.
  • Kindness: It’s amazing how you remember and pray for so many people. It shows your kind and good heart.

And of course, we can’t forget your love for food! You’ve always shown how to enjoy a meal like a king and serve others with care. And yes — your little dessert tricks are the best: “No no, I’ve only had one... okay maybe one last time!” 😄 (Teaching us negotiation skills to always get more and desire more!)
